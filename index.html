Impact on smallhoder farmers in Ghana
The production of Ready-to-Use Therapeutic Food (RUTF) creates a significant positive impact on smallholder farmers by providing them with a sustainable source of income
By sourcing raw materials like peanuts, and other essential ingredients directly from local farmers, we empower them economically and foster long-term partnerships
Impact on the climate and environment
Our approach to RUTF production prioritizes sustainability and environmental stewardship. By supporting local farming communities and minimizing supply chain distances, we reduce our carbon footprint and contribute to climate resilience
Impact on the health and nutrition of children
RUTF is a life-saving solution designed to combat severe malnutrition in children, ensuring they receive essential nutrients for growth and development. Our products are carefully formulated to provide high-quality, nutrient-dense food that meets international standards for malnutrition treatment
